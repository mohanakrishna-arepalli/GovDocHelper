Instructions for Obtaining NREGA Job Card

Eligibility:

To obtain an NREGA job card, you should be eligible under the government's NREGA employment scheme.

Application Process:

Contact the responsible NREGA department or authority in your area for job card issuance. Submit necessary documents for processing and fulfill any required procedures.

Verification:

Your application and documents will be verified by the NREGA authority to ensure eligibility and accuracy of the provided information.

Issuance of Job Card:

Once verified, the NREGA job card will be issued to you. It includes all essential details regarding your NREGA entitlements and payment process.

Wage Disbursement:

Your wages under the NREGA scheme will be disbursed according to eligibility and process specified in the scheme.
