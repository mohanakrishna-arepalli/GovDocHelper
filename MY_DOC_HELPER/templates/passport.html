Significance and Use of Passport

A Passport is an essential document issued by the government that allows an individual to travel internationally. It serves as proof of identity and nationality. Its uses include:

  • International travel
  • Proof of identity and nationality
  • Verification for visa applications
  • Identification in foreign countries

Instructions for Filling Passport Form

  1. Register on the Passport Seva Portal: Visit the Passport Seva portal and register with your personal details to create an account.
  2. Fill in all the Required Details Accurately:
    • Personal Information:
      • Full Name: Enter your full name as per your supporting documents.
      • Date of Birth: Provide your date of birth in the format DD/MM/YYYY.
      • Gender: Select your gender (Male, Female, or Other) as applicable.
      • Address: Provide your current residential address, including house number, street name, locality, city, district, state, and PIN code.
    • Additional Information:
      • Contact Information: Provide your mobile number and email address (if applicable).
      • Aadhar Number: Enter your Aadhar number if available.
      • Parent/Guardian Information: Provide details of your parents or guardians as required.
  3. Submit the Form: Once you have filled in all the required details, submit the form online through the Passport Seva portal. You will receive an application reference number (ARN).
  4. Pay the Fee: Pay the required fee online using the available payment options.
  5. Book an Appointment: Schedule an appointment at the nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Regional Passport Office (RPO).
  6. Visit the PSK/RPO: Visit the PSK/RPO on the scheduled date with the required documents for verification and biometrics.
  7. Track Application Status: Track the status of your application online using the ARN.
  8. Collect Your Passport: After the verification process is complete, you will receive your Passport by post.
