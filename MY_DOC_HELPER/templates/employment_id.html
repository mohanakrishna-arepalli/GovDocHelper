Employment Identity Card

Significance and Use of Employment Identity Card

The employment identity card serves as a proof of employment and is issued by employers to their employees. It is used for various purposes including:

  • Accessing restricted areas within the workplace
  • Identifying oneself as an employee of the organization
  • Participating in company events and activities
  • Availing employee discounts and benefits
  • Verifying employment status for official purposes

Instructions for Obtaining Employment Identity Card

  1. Contact Human Resources (HR): Reach out to your organization's HR department to inquire about the process for obtaining an employment identity card.
  2. Submit Required Documents: Provide any necessary documents or information as requested by the HR department.
  3. Photo Session: Attend a photo session arranged by the organization for capturing your image for the identity card.
  4. Card Issuance: Once the necessary steps are completed, your employment identity card will be issued by the organization.
