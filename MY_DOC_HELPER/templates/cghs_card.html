CGHS Card

Eligibility:

To obtain a CGHS card, you need to be eligible under the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS). CGHS provides comprehensive medical care facilities to Central Government employees and their dependents.

Application Process:

To apply for a CGHS card, visit the CGHS website or the nearest CGHS Wellness Centre. Submit the required documents and complete any necessary formalities.

Verification:

Your application and documents will be verified by the CGHS authorities to ensure eligibility and accuracy of the information provided.

Card Issuance:

Once verified, your CGHS card will be issued to you. It contains all the essential details regarding your CGHS entitlements and payment procedures.

Benefits:

The CGHS card provides access to a wide range of medical services, including outpatient treatment, specialist consultations, diagnostic tests, and hospitalization, at CGHS empanelled hospitals and dispensaries.
