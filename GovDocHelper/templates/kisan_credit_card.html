Kisan Credit Card

Kisan Credit Card Information

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) is a special credit card offered to farmers in India. It aims to provide them with timely and adequate credit support for their agricultural needs. The card provides farmers with access to short-term credit for crop production, post-harvest expenses, marketing activities, and other agricultural requirements.

Instructions for Filling Kisan Credit Card Application

  1. Download the Kisan Credit Card Application Form:

    2. Visit the nearest bank or cooperative society that offers Kisan Credit Cards to obtain the application form. You can also download the form from the bank's official website.

  2. Fill in all the Required Details Accurately:
    • Personal Information: Provide your name, address, contact details, and other required personal information.
    • Landholding Details: Mention the details of your landholding, including size, location, and type of cultivation.
    • Income Details: Provide information about your income from agriculture and other sources.
    • Requested Loan Amount: Specify the amount of credit you require for agricultural purposes.
    • Purpose of Credit: State the purpose for which you need the credit, such as crop production, farm equipment purchase, or other agricultural activities.
  3. Submit the Form:

    4. After filling the form, submit it along with the required documents to the bank or cooperative society. Ensure all details are accurate and complete.

  4. Verification and Approval:

    5. The bank will verify your application and documents. Once approved, you will receive your Kisan Credit Card with the sanctioned credit limit.
